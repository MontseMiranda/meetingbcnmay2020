Hotel Olivia Plaza

The Olivia Plaza Hotel is a magnificent boutique hotel in the center of Barcelona. A superior four-star hotel on Plaça Catalunya with free Wi-Fi, garden, restaurant, parking, terraces and meeting rooms. Plus, our guests also have access to the Holmes Place Urquinaona gym. Olivia Plaza is a hotel with cutting-edge design, loads of sunlight, designed for your comfort and convenience.

This hotel has 113 rooms with views of Plaça Catalunya or the Gothic Quarter, for an exclusive, unforgettable stay in Barcelona. Plus, all of our rooms are soundproofed to make sure guests get their rest despite being in the heart of the city center.
